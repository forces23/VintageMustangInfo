A little Background

Around the end of 1964 Ford wanted to build a car that could compete with the popular European sports cars and also the American Chevorolette Corvette Sting Ray. When the design team first came up with this car, it was given the name "T-5". This car was determined to be a cross breed between the Roadster and the Ford Falcon because of the popular demands of the time period. Before it was release the name was changed from "T-5" to the "Mustang". At first the was only in hard top at first then they made convertible and later on the design a fastback Mustang for a more sleek and sporty design that really mad the car pop.
